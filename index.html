About Me

I am a researcher working at the intersection of rigorous mathematical theory and data science applications. Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Decision Sciences Department at the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB). Prior to this, I was involved in data-driven statistical modeling frameworks at Sorbonne University Abu Dhabi.

My core research focuses on data-driven statistical modeling for spatial scaling in Extreme Value Theory (EVT) and its real-world applications to climate variables (such as rainfall and temperature) and infrastructure resilience (such as power grids).
